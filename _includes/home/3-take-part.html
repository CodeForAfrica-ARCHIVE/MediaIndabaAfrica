Be part of
Media Party Africa

Media Party Africa is hosted by the continent’s largest data journal- ism and civic technology federation, Code for Africa, in partnership with Hacks/Hackers Africa. Together, we're gathering some of the world’s most exciting civic technologists, digital journalists and other social justice watchdogs from across the world for three days of workshops and talks. Media Party Africa is modelled on the original Media Party, in Buenos Aires, which has been taking place annually for the last five years

This is the first year that it will be held in Africa, in one of the continent’s tech & design capitals, Cape Town.
