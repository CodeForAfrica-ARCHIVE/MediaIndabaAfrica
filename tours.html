If you’re attending the GIJN conference in Johannesburg until 19 November, and would like to make use of the gap between then and the start of Media Party Africa on the 23rd, we’ve sourced a few tours you might be interested in. Here’s a sample - if you’d like something specific, please mail Zea Tomlinson, or call her on +27 21 4246644

> Option 1

Cape Town to Knysna 3 Days / 2 Nights

Rate: R9680.00 per person
Departs every day from your hotel or guesthouse in the Cape Town area at 07:30am.
HIGHLIGHTS

Day 1: Oudtshoorn

The charming town of Oudtshoorn, situated in the Klein Karoo is home to South Africa’s ostrich farm industry and the world famous Cango Cave
• Wine Tasting Robertson
• Visit the Ostrich farm
Dinner & overnight stay at Turnberry Boutique Hotel or Similar.

Day 2: Knysna / Plettenberg Bay

Knysna, situated between lush forests and the shores of a peaceful lagoon offers a wide variety of activities and attractions.
• Cango Caves
• Swartberg Pass
• Lunch at "Kobus se Gat"
• Knysna Heads and Waterfront
• Elephant Park (optional)
Dinner & overnight stay in Graywood Hotel or Similar

Day 3: Mossel Bay

Depart from your hotel to Mossel Bay. Venture on a 4X4 Safari, one of the biggest attractions on the Garden route.
• Visit Mossel
• 4X4 Safari (duration 2h)
Return to Cape Town in the afternoon

> Option 2

Cape Town to Knysna 3 Days / 2 Nights

Rate: R13150.00 per person
Departs from your hotel or guesthouse in the Cape Town area at 07:30am.
HIGHLIGHTS

Day 1: Oudtshoorn

Depart from your hotel in Cape Town and travel via Robertson winelands. Visit the oldest and biggest cheetah and croc contact centre world- wide.
• Wine tasting on route to Oudtshoorn in Robertson area
• Ostrich farm
Dinner & overnight stay in Oudtshoorn.

Day 2: Oudtshoorn/ Knysna

Breakfast
Visit the charming town of Oudtshoorn,,home to South Africa’s ostrich farm industry and the world famous Cango Caves as well as Knysna, situated between lush forests and the shores of a peaceful lagoon, offering a wide variety of activities and attractions.
• Cango Caves
• Elephant Park (optional)
Dinner & overnight stay in Knysna.

Day 3: Tsitsikamma / Plettenberg Bay

Breakfast
Tsitsikamma is an area characterised by large tracts of indigenous forests, deep river gorges and magnificent coastlines.
• Nature's Valley
• Monkeyland & Birds of Eden (optional)
• Tsitsikamma Nature Reserve
• Plettenberg Bay
Dinner & overnight stay in Knysna

Day 4: Mossel Bay

Breakfast
Mossel Bay - discovered by Bartholomew Dias, Mossel Bay is one of the biggest attractions on the Garden Route.
• Knysna Heads and Waterfront
• Safari tour at Game Reserve
Return to Cape Town
