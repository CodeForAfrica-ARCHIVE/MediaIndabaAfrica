SOUTHERN SUN THE CULLINAN

The Cullinan offers accommodation in the heart of Cape Town, 400 metres from this year’s Media Party Africa venue, Portside Tower. The hotel is close to the V&A Waterfront, the Bree St restaurant and shopping strip, Long Street and other popular landmarks.
Guests have access to a host of services and amenities, including the large outdoor heated pool with its spacious deck, a fully equipped fitness centre, and a damn fine view of the city.

STAYEASY CAPE TOWN

With modern amenities, warm hospitality, a perfect location and a price that’s easy on the pocket, StayEasy Cape Town is an easy choice when deciding where to stay for Media Party Africa 2017.
Located on the vibrant corner of Bree and Strand Street, the hotel is 450 metres from you Portside, this year’s Media Party venue, and close to the city’s shopping district and cultural sites. StayEasy’s modern lobby is particularly appealing to guests who want to meet, relax or work in a comfortable environment. This space has great high-speed Wi-Fi connectivity.

THE SOUTHERN SUN WATERFRONT

Southern Sun Waterfront Hotel in Cape Town is located across the road from Portside, the venue for Media Party Africa 2017.
Stylish and inviting, the hotel provides comfortable and convenient accommodation for delegates attending the event. Guests are surrounded by world famous attractions including Cape Town’s Green Point Stadium, Table Mountain and the V&A Waterfront.
Recently refurbished, the award-winning hotel offers beautifully-appointed rooms, with a host of amenities and services on offer, including free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a fully-equipped fitness centre and an outdoor pool. Dining options include South African inspired dishes at the hotel’s Yizani Restaurant and cocktails at the Waterfront Bar.

THE RADISSON BLU HOTEL & RESIDENCE

The newly opened Radisson Blu Hotel & Residence is less than three blocks from this year’s Media Party Africa venue. The hotel boasts award-winning architecture and is an iconic landmark on the Cape Town skyline. The modern rooms have views of the city, mountain, harbour or sea, and come with amenities like a Smart TV and Wi-Fi.
